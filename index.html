Geographic Trends in Wine Production - 2010 to 2014

The global wine market has long been dominated by the traditional wine-making countries of Europe: France, Italy, and Spain. And while those countries continue to trade spots atop the podium for greatest volume of wine produced annualy, some surprising nations are getting in the grape-growing game! Explore the data gathered by the International Organization of Wine and Vine and see if you can guess where the next vinticulture craze will turn up!
